BEST FINDS INDIA AI

AI-powered affiliate marketing automation platform for creating, managing and publishing product content to Pinterest Business and Telegram.

About

BEST FINDS INDIA AI is a personal automation application developed to simplify affiliate marketing workflows. The application uses Artificial Intelligence to generate product captions, descriptions and promotional content before publishing them to the owner's Pinterest Business account and Telegram channel. The application is intended only for the owner's business workflow and is not a public consumer application.

Features

  • AI Product Analysis
  • AI Caption Generation
  • Pinterest Content Publishing
  • Telegram Channel Publishing
  • Affiliate Marketing Automation
  • Gemini AI Integration
  • Product Workflow Management
